# Platform Rules & Character Limits

## Twitter/X
- **Limit:** 280 characters per tweet
- **Hashtags:** Max 2 per tweet, avoid in first tweets
- **Links:** Count against character limit (t.co wrapper = 23 chars)
- **Thread:** Connect with ↳ or numbered format
- **Best times to post:** 8-9 AM and 12-1 PM local time
- **Hook formulas:** "The #1 mistake...", "Nobody talks about...", "I spent 100 hours researching..."

## LinkedIn
- **Limit:** 3000 characters per post
- **First line:** Critical — must stop the scroll within 2 seconds
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 max, at the end or in comments
- **CTA:** "Thoughts?" or "Link in comments" to drive engagement
- **Format:** Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences), line breaks between each
- **Best times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7-8 AM or 5-6 PM local

## Reddit
- **Title limit:** 300 characters
- **Format:** No clickbait, be specific, include the insight in the title
- **Self-promotion:** Max 10% of your posts — lead with value
- **Karma:** Start with helpful comments before posting your own content
- **Subreddits to consider:** r/Entrepreneur, r/SideProject, r/startups, r/technology

## Email Newsletter
- **Subject line:** <50 characters, curiosity over clever
- **Preview text:** 85-100 characters, one sentence
- **Body:** 150-300 words ideal for most newsletters
- **CTA:** One clear ask per email
- **Format:** Plain text feel with clear sections

## General Tips
- Never copy-paste the same content everywhere — each platform rewards native content
- Include a unique insight or angle for each platform
- Data/stats perform well on all platforms — lead with numbers
- Images increase engagement significantly on all platforms
