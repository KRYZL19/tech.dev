## Proven 3 AI Side Hustle Models (2026)

### 1. Local Business Automation
- **Stack:** HighLevel ($97/mo) + ChatGPT ($20/mo)
- **Revenue:** $300–500/month recurring per client
- **Examples:** Booking bots for wellness studios, dental offices, real estate lead qualification
- **Key stat:** A wellness studio owner paid $400/month for a ChatGPT booking bot that took 10 min to build
- **Time to first dollar:** 2–4 weeks (outreach is bottleneck)
- **Overlap with our service:** We're doing EXACTLY this — setup-as-a-service for local businesses

### 2. Digital Products
- **Revenue:** $5–50/sale, unlimited volume after creation
- **Examples:** Prompt templates for real estate agents, Notion dashboards, Canva templates with AI copy
- **Time to first dollar:** 1–3 months (audience building required)
- **Overlap with our service:** We could package OpenClaw setup guides + prompt packs as digital products

### 3. Niche Consulting
- **Revenue:** $50–200/hour coaching
- **Key stat:** A psychologist teaching therapists AI for session notes commands $150/hour
- **Time to first dollar:** 1–2 weeks (if you have domain expertise)
- **Overlap:** Could offer OpenClaw consulting for specific verticals
