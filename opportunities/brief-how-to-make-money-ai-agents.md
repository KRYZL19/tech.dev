### H2: Path 4 — Agent-Native Micro-SaaS (Highest Ceiling, Highest Risk)
**H3: What Makes a SaaS "Agent-Native"**
- The agent IS the product's core functionality
- vs. traditional SaaS where AI is a support feature

**H3: Viable Agent-Native Micro-SaaS Ideas**
- Automated research report (weekly niche digest, $10–30/mo)
- Social content scheduling bot ($29–99/mo)
- Competitor monitoring service ($20–50/mo per competitor)
- Personalized cold email outreach agent ($200–500/mo)

**H3: Economics of an Agent-Native SaaS**
- Cost floor: VPS ($5–20) + LLM API ($10–200) + Stripe (2.9% + 30¢) + email ($5–20)
- Total: $30–250/month for a small operation
- Breakeven math: 3 customers at $30/month = breakeven
- Scaling challenge: compute costs grow with active users

**H3: Timeline to Revenue**
| Phase | Timeline | MRR |
|---|---|---|
| Build MVP + launch | Months 1–3 | $0–150 |
| PMF validation | Months 4–6 | $150–500 |
| Growth | Months 7–12 | $600–$2,500 |
| Year 2 | Months 12+ | $2,000–$7,500 |
